Fakelos Party

There are a total of 12 rounds, every round a game from the ones below is randomly selected. Each game is different in terms of how it's played but also in terms of points given.

Draw my thing!

Everyone is given the same sentence and they have 90 seconds to draw it as best as they can. After the 90 seconds have passed, or if everyone finishes before that, the drawings of every player are shown and you have to vote for the best.

Every vote counts for 600 points.
If a player gets more than 2 or more votes, they get an extra 100 points.
If a player gets all the votes(except for his own), he gets an extra 400 points.

Fill the blank!

Everyone is given the same sentence with a part of the sentence missing. Each player can fill the blank and later, everyone votes for the funniest answer according to them.

Every vote counts for 600 points.
If a player gets more than 2 or more votes, they get an extra 100 points.
If a player gets all the votes(except for his own), he gets an extra 400 points.

Typeracer!

A list of 20 randomly selected words are given to each player. Then he has to type as many words as possible in the set amount of time.

Every player gets 100 points for every word typed.
Every player loses 40 points for every word they didn't type.

Memorize the pattern!

The same table with 36 panels is shown to every player, 12 panels are black, 24 are not. In the given time you have to memorize the pattern and later fill the empty table.

Every player gets 100 points for every correct panel.
Every player loses 80 points for every wrong panel.

Guess my drawing!

A random player is selected to draw a random word in 90 seconds. Every other player has to guess what the word is.

Every player that guesses the word gets 600 points.
The artist gains 600 points if 1 person guesses correctly, and 100 points for every extra player.

Geomaster!

A random location in the word is shown to every player, and everyone has to guess where that location could be on the map.

The players are sorted based on how close they got to the correct location.
The player closest gains 900 points.
The second player gets 900 - 140 points.
The third player gets 900 - 2 * 140 points.
etc.

Pick a card!

5 different cards are shown to the players, each one giving X amount of points. Each player picks one card.

Card One gives -200 points.
Card Two gives -100 points.
Card Three gives 0 points.
Card Four gives 200 points.
Card Five gives 1000 points.
If two or more players pick the same card, they lose 500 points for every player that picked it!

Dodgeball!

Each player has 5 lives total, and has to dodge and not get hit by the balls for 30 seconds.

5 lives left 1400 points.
4 lives left 1100 points.
3 lives left 800 points.
2 lives left 500 points.
1 lives left 200 points.
0 lives left -100 points.
